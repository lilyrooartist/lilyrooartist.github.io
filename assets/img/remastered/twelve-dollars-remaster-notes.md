# Twelve Dollars Cover Remaster Notes

Generated on June 7, 2026 from the original text-bearing Twelve Dollars direction, now archived at `_archive/2026-06-07-album-art-cleanup/assets-img/Twelve Dollars.jpeg`.

## Direction

- Remove all album/artist text from the cover.
- Preserve the core image language: empty red stage, curtains, shoes, scattered dollars, theatrical lights.
- Produce release-oriented square artwork that can sit beside other Lily Roo covers without baked-in title text.
