Site Policy

Privacy Policy

Lily Roo keeps the site lightweight and collects only what is needed to receive messages, send requested updates, and operate the public website.

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Information You Provide

If you join the mailing list or use the contact form, you may provide an email address and, for contact requests, a message. That information is used to send Lily Roo updates you requested or to respond to your message.

Service Providers

The contact and mailing-list forms use Google Forms as the form backend. When you submit those forms, the information you enter is sent to Google Forms for storage and processing. Links to YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, X, PayPal, and other third-party services are governed by those services' own privacy policies.

Cookies And Analytics

The public website does not intentionally set advertising cookies or run behavioral ad tracking. Third-party embeds and outbound services, such as YouTube or payment links, may use their own cookies or similar technologies once you interact with them.

How Information Is Used

  • To send requested release updates, broadcast notices, and related Lily Roo communications.
  • To respond to direct messages submitted through the contact form.
  • To maintain, troubleshoot, and protect the website and social publishing workflow.

Your Choices

You can ignore optional forms, stop using the site, or use unsubscribe links included in mailing-list communications. For privacy questions or deletion requests, use the contact form.

Children

This site is intended for a general audience and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the Lily Roo contact form.
