# Brain Rot — Image Prompt Pack (10)

Use these for cover art, reels backgrounds, quote cards, and short promo visuals.

1. **Neon Overload Portrait**
   - A moody portrait in a dark room lit only by phone glow and neon UI fragments, subtle scanlines, grain, anxious expression, indie album aesthetic.

2. **Tabs Crashing Collage**
   - Surreal collage of 50 browser tabs exploding into paper scraps around a person laughing and panicking at once, punk zine style.

3. **Doomscroll Hands**
   - Close-up hands scrolling endlessly, reflected in tired eyes, soft cinematic light, emotional realism.

4. **Signal vs Static Split**
   - Split image: left side calm analog textures (tape hiss, paper notes), right side digital static overload, center silhouette of artist.

5. **Midnight Feed Confessional**
   - Bedroom at 2:13 AM, laptop + phone + empty coffee cup, handwritten lyric notes on floor, intimate indie documentary look.

6. **Satire Smile / Sad Eyes**
   - Pop-art style character smiling with comic speech bubbles while eyes show exhaustion; bright colors with dark undertone.

7. **Notification Storm**
   - Abstract visual storm of notification icons swirling like weather around a lone figure in hoodie, cinematic, high contrast.

8. **Brainwave Glitch**
   - Stylized head silhouette with waveform and glitch artifacts replacing thoughts, purple/blue neon palette, modern alt music branding.

9. **After Screen-Off Silence**
   - A dark room seconds after all screens turn off, only faint moonlight and static texture, melancholic and quiet.

10. **Folk Punk Digital Ruin**
   - Folk-punk poster style: rough textures, torn paper, marker typography “BRAIN ROT”, mixed with QR code/static motifs.
