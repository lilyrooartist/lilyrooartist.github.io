## Video pan direction
- For the YouTube video, render a true 16:9 landscape frame that fills the screen.
- Pan upward from the lower tea setting to the top of the tiered platter.
- Keep the stand centered throughout the move so the viewer always understands the subject.
- End on the formal top tier, silver handle, floral detail, and airy tea-room backdrop.
- Avoid spilled cups, knocked-over service, smoke, fog, heavy steam, surreal distortions, messy table styling, logos, LR branding, earrings, visible ear jewelry, blond highlights, watermark, title lettering, readable phone content, and a fully visible face.
