- **Song ID:** LR-SONG-022

## Core hook

Spilling the Tea should read as formal afternoon tea service: composed, ritualized, and socially loaded. The title is the phrase, not an instruction to show a spill.

## Anecdote hooks

1. The tea service turns gossip into ceremony: polished silver, careful plates, and a whole room pretending not to listen.
2. The tension comes from etiquette. Everything looks proper, but the conversation underneath is doing the work.
3. The visual world should feel bright, formal, and controlled instead of smoky, messy, or surreal.
